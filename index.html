Curriculum Vitae

Zhaoyu Hu is currently a Ph.D. student from the Department of Electronic Engineering, Fudan University under the supervision of Prof. Jinhua Yu.
Previously, he got both his B.S. from the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering, North University of China in 2019. His personal social websites are Linkedin | Twitter | Github.

Zhaoyu Hu is boardly interested in medical image computing, computer vision and machine learning. Currently, Zhaoyu Hu is working on MR images normalization based on deep learning.
